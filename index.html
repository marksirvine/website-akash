Masters Thesis


I asked the question "Can you use artificial neural networks to reduce perceptual issues in augmented reality (AR)?".

I found that in using a convolutional neural network I was able to create images and videos where AR objects were very difficult to distinguish from real ones.
The network was able to replace the low level features of the AR image (where perceptual issues were present) with consistent and homogeneous features.

The image to the left contains an AR pear and AR red pen.
